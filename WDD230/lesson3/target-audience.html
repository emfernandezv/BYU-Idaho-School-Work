Target Audience

The desired audience for this site is our Kirkland, Washington community, we aim to reach everybody in our area and look for possible new business owners that want to be part of the Chamber. We also want to reach residents who want to support our local business community.

Personas

  • Young Kirkland residents feel they have to support local businesses by purchasing in their places instead of driving miles away to get the same product in a bigger store. They don't know what businesses are in the area.
  • A new entrepreneur opens a new store and is looking for visibility around the area to push the business but doesn't know how to reach more people or where to display special discounts for buyers in the area.

Scenarious

  • Where can I have lunch today?
  • Where can I purchase handcrafted jewelry?
  • How can I make my business more visible?
  • Where can I share my business promotions?
  • What alcohol-free bars are around the area?
  • Is there any south american convinience store near by?
